About Us!

Our Ethos

GourmetToGo’s ethos is to produce gourmet, affordable food sourcing local ingredients and using local suppliers where possible. Established in 2006 with a restaurant in Dun Laoghaire, there are now 6 locations across Dublin and Galway including a state-of-the-art catering kitchen in Santry.

Flavour is (almost) everything

We believe in flavour. But we also believe in brilliant service and incredible food. Bring all three together, and you transform fantastic food into a fantastic experience, which is what we’ve been doing since we first opened our doors in 2006.

The Gourmet Team

Lewis Bambrick is Founder and CEO of GourmetToGo. Lewis is the definition of ambitious, he is tenacious, resilient and not afraid of hard work. Hailing from a small village in Co. Galway, he studied at the University College Dublin before heading off to New York for three years, working in a diverse range of food businesses. On returning to Dublin, he opened the first restaurant in Dun Laoghaire in 2006. Since then, GourmetToGo has grown into a very successful business employing over 200 people across its seven locations.
